🤖 To-Do Agent Workflow

How action items are collected, stored, and shown to you

📡  Step 1 — Data Sources
🎯
Jira
Assigned tickets & comment @mentions
💬
Slack
Channel @mentions & DMs
📧
Gmail
Google Docs/Sheets comment notifications
📄
Google Drive
Files where you are mentioned
🤖  Step 2 — /collect-todo Agent
1
Load — reads data.json and notes all existing item IDs
2
Fetch — queries all 4 sources in parallel, extracts the ask and timestamp
3
Filter — skips duplicates, resolved tickets, and bot notifications
4
Prioritise — 🔴 blocking/urgent  ·  🟡 assigned task  ·  🟢 FYI
5
Merge — multiple people in same Slack thread → one item
6
Write — merges new items into data.json and saves ACTION_ITEMS.md
💾  Step 3 — data.json (Persistent Store)
items[ ]
Active items — sorted high → medium → low
archivedItems[ ]
Completed items — newest first
lastSynced
Timestamp of the last /collect-todo run
🖥️  Step 4 — Next.js App (localhost:3000)
GET /api/items Reads data.json and sends it to the browser
PATCH /api/items/:id Complete → archive  |  Restore → active (re-sorted)
POST /api/refresh Re-reads data.json — picks up latest sync
🔄  Browser auto-polls GET /api/items every 3 minutes in the background
👤  Step 5 — You
🖱️
Click a card — opens the source link (Jira ticket, Slack thread, or Google Sheet)
✅
Tick the checkbox — marks it done and moves it to the Archive tab
↩️
Restore from archive — moves it back to Active, auto-sorted by priority
🔄
Refresh button — manually reloads data.json after running /collect-todo
